Scope of Work

Marine & Boating

Our marine division specializes in custom fabrication that you can't buy off the shelf. We manufacture duplicate windshields for center consoles and walk-arounds, often saving customers weeks of wait time compared to OEM parts. We also fabricate custom Starboard dash panels, replace rotted hatch covers, and repair aluminum T-tops and towers.

  • Custom Acrylic Windshields
  • Polycarbonate Enclosures
  • Aluminum Welding Repairs

Industrial & Trailer

Beyond the water, we keep industry moving. We fabricate safety guards for manufacturing equipment, custom fluid tanks, and heavy-duty brackets. Our trailer repair shop handles everything from simple bunk replacements to complete axle swaps and structural frame welding for boat and utility trailers.

  • Machine Guards & Covers
  • Trailer Axles & Suspension
  • Structural Steel & Aluminum
